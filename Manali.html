Solang Valley

Solang Valley is a paradise for thrill-seekers.
You can enjoy paragliding, zorbing, skiing, and ropeways here.

Hidimba Temple

Hadimba Temple is dedicated to Hadimba Devi (from the Mahabharata).
Its wooden architecture and peaceful surroundings make it a spiritual and historical gem in Manali.

Jogini Waterfall

A short trek from Vashisht Temple leads you to this serene and beautiful waterfall.
Surrounded by lush greenery and mountain views, Jogini Falls is a great place to relax, meditate,
or just enjoy a picnic in nature.
