Rajasthan

Rajasthan, the "Land of Kings," offers an unparalleled historical 
experience with its magnificent forts, opulent palaces, 
and rich cultural heritage.


Jaisalmer Fort

This "Golden Fort" is one of the world's few living forts, 
with narrow winding lanes housing temples, havelis, and 
shops within its massive yellow sandstone walls.


Amber Fort

This majestic 16th-century hilltop fort features stunning 
Rajput architecture with ornate gateways, intricate mirror work 
in the Sheesh Mahal (Palace of Mirrors), and spectacular 
views of Maota Lake.


Chittorgarh Fort

India's largest fort complex spanning 700 acres, 
known for its tragic history of valor and three famous sieges 
where Rajput women committed jauhar (mass self-immolation).
